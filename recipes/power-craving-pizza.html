Power Cravings Pizza

Description:

Mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, italian sausage, and MORE cheese!

Ingredients:

  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Italian pepperoni
  • Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Romano cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Flour
  • 1 Egg

Steps:

  1. First mix the flour with 1 Egg to create the base dough.
  2. Then let dough sit for one hour covered with cheese sheet.
  3. Flatten dough into a round shape.
  4. Start tossing round dough into the air while rotating it to maintain pizza shape.
  5. Spread tomato sauce evenly all around dough.
  6. Add the Mozzarella cheese, Romano cheese, and Parmesan cheese all grated.
  7. Add pepperoni and sausages.
  8. Bake in oven at 400 degrees for 45 mins.
  9. Bake until golden crust and enjoy!
