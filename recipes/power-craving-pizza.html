Power Cravings Pizza

Description:

Mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, italian sausage, and MORE cheese!

Ingredients:

  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Italian pepperoni
  • Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Romano cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Flour
  • 1 Egg

Steps:

  1. First mix the flour with 1 Egg to create the base dough.
  2. Then let dough sit for one hour covered with cheese sheet.
